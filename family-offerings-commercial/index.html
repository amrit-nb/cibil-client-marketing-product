Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Premium Duo Subscription Plan?

The Premium Duo plan offers a discounted annual Premium Subscription for two members. When you upgrade to the Premium Duo plan, you receive full access to all Premium subscription benefits, along with a 100% discount gift code that you can share with a known business associate of another company to activate the same plan at no cost.

How to use the gift code included in my Premium Duo plan?

Share the one-time-use gift code with your known business associate of another company. They can apply it as a 100% discount code while purchasing CIBIL Premium Subscription plan — whether they are new or an existing CIBIL account user.

What is the validity of the gift code included in my Premium Duo plan?

100% discount gift code received with Premium Duo subscription plan is valid for 1 year from the date of purchase.

Where can I find my gift code from Premium Duo plan?

You can view your gift code, its validity (1 year from the date of purchase of Premium Duo Plan), and status under the following section post login:

Report Outline → Overview → Your Gift Code
