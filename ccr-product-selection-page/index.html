I could not complete the GSTIN-based authentication during the request process. Can I still access my GST Report?

Yes, you can still access your CIBIL Rank and Company Credit Report even if GSTIN authentication was not completed. However, the GST Report will only be available once GSTIN authentication is successfully completed.

I do not wish to access the GST Report for my business. Do I still need to provide GSTIN during the sign-up process?

No, providing GSTIN is optional during sign-up. If you do not wish to access the GST Report, you may skip the GSTIN step. You will still be able to access your CIBIL Rank and Company Credit Report.

I do not have a GSTIN for my business. Will I be able to access CIBIL Rank and Company Credit Report?

Yes. GSTIN is not mandatory to access your CIBIL Rank and Company Credit Report. The GST Report is an additional feature available to businesses with a valid GSTIN.

How can I refresh the Company Credit Report, CIBIL Rank and GST Report?

Log in to your dashboard and click on the "Refresh" button to view your refreshed reports. Your CIBIL Rank and Company Credit Report can be refreshed every 7 days during the validity of the subscription. You can refresh your GST Report once every month for the duration of the subscription plan. Please note that you will need to complete the GSTIN-based authentication every time you refresh your GST Report.

Can I download the copy of GST Report?

Yes, you can download a copy of your business' GST Report from your dashboard.
