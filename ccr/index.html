Company Credit Report (CCR)

CIBIL CCR is a record of your company’s credit history, created from data submitted to CIBIL by lending institutions across India.

The past payment behavior of a company is a strong indication of its future behavior. It is, therefore, important to understand that the CCR is heavily relied on by loan providers to evaluate and approve loan applications.

CIBIL Rank

CIBIL RANK, summarizes your CCR in the form of one number. The rank is similar to the CIBIL Score provided for individuals.

  • Ranges from a scale of 1 to 10. The closer your rank is to 1, the better are your chances of securing a loan.
  • CIBIL Rank is available for commercial borrowers with a credit exposure up to INR 50 crores.
  • Indicates the repayment behavior of your company which is one of the key factors considered by a lender while evaluating a loan application.

I do not have a GSTIN for my business. Will I be able to access CIBIL Rank and Company Credit Report?

If your business does not have a GSTIN, you can still go ahead and complete the request process without the GSTIN. Instead, you will need to upload identity documents to authenticate your request, and you will receive a notification once your documents have been verified. After this, you will be able to view your CIBIL Rank and CCR.

How can I refresh the Company Credit Report and CIBIL Rank ?

Log in to your dashboard and click on the “Refresh” button to view your refreshed reports. Your CIBIL Rank and Company Credit Report can be refreshed every 7 days during the validity of the subscription.
